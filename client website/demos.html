Demo Games

Interested in a game but not certain that you’ll enjoy it? Looking for something new to play? Then you are in the perfect place!

At Here Be Books & Games, we want you to have fun and to expand your horizons as a gamer. To that end, we have spent years building up a sizable collection of board, dice, and card games that you can try out without first having to spend money. It’s the best way to avoid buyer’s remorse, and we’ll even sit down with you and teach you how to play. There’s no better way to learn a game than by getting hands-on and playing it yourself.

We know board games. We enjoy board games, and we want you to enjoy them too. We’ve played most of the games we stock on our shelves, so we can make good recommendations based on your tastes. It will take the guesswork out of buying new games and save you money as well. That sounds like a win all around.

You’re also welcome to participate in our free Learn to Play events and Open Gaming every Saturday (except the big monthly Game Night). You can see what games we’ll be spotlighting in our monthly newsletter. It includes descriptions of the games, game reviews, upcoming events, and more.

Curious to know if we have a demo copy of a specific game? That’s no problem. Just ask us, or click to see our complete list on Board Game Geek.
